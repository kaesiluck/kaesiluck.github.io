SIR (susceptible-infected-recovered) Vaccination Model, with Vital Dynamics.

Using standard mathematical modeling principles, I created a project that shows the steady-state
for an infection that is introduced, with and without the presence of vaccines. By making educated assumptions,
creating appropriate variables and constants, performing a dimension check and non-parameterization, I was able to determine the
corresponding eigenvalues that were necessary in solving for the steady-state circumstances.
